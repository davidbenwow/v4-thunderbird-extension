How it works:

  • The V4 toolbar icon shows an orange border when the message contains leads that need marking in V4.
  • Click the icon to see them, then Mark Lead in V4 to open the lead's page in your browser where you can mark response, manuscript, or rejected.
  • The extension also scans the quoted thread — useful when a prospect replies from a different address.
  • You need to be logged into v4.vdm-vsg.de in your browser for the Mark button to work.
